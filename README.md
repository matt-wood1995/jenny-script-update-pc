## What This Mod Does

Jenny Mod adds a persistent NPC companion to Minecraft Forge 1.20 that behaves far beyond simple mobs or trading villagers. The character tracks your conversations, reacts to your in-game actions, and evolves its relationship with you over time. It supports multiple languages, keeps a video diary of your adventures, and adjusts its replies based on your play style and choices.

The 2026 version overhauls the companion's AI core, delivering more subtle emotional reactions and a modular settings system. You can tune the experience to your liking—choose a quiet partner for late-night mining or a chatty friend for building sessions. 1. **Download** the mod `.jar` from the link above.

3. **Move** the `.jar` file into your `mods` folder.

Example summon command:

## Configuration Options

| Setting | Default | Description |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| `companion.enableNightMode` | `true` | Activates special behavior during nighttime |
| `companion.language` | `auto` | Automatic language detection or manual selection |
| `companion.interactionFrequency` | `medium` | How often the NPC starts a conversation |
| `companion.videoJournal` | `true` | Toggles automatic recording of highlights |
| `companion.emotionalMemory` | `true` | Remembers past events and adjusts replies |

Settings are stored in `config/jennymod.toml` after the first game launch.
